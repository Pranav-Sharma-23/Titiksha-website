Lift It Up

The power of fluid can is such that it can move the world. From generating electricity to applying brakes in vehicle fluid has become a part of our day to day life. Titiksha offers you a chance to go hand in hand with the rising demands of fluid power in the industry. The event focuses on lifting loads from one platform to another with the help of a self-made arm and moving it with the help of syringes filled with water. The one with most accuracy and speed bags away the prize. Max. Participants: 3 per team.


WRIGGLERS

Man has always used wheels for its machines to move. But nature employs no such measure when it comes to locomotion. It has been a hobby for a number of years to develop a bicycle without wheels that could walk. It would move on legs and resemble a large insect. Here is your chance to take the challenge with your entourage and excel to compete the nature. Design and prototype a link mechanism to resemble any living organism like spider and move it to some distance. Excellency in motion and stability of the link is what you got to watch out for.
Max Participants: 3 per team

Rush to Assemble Engine

A mechanical engineer is incomplete without engine. It is an engine which moves the world. The IC engine has become a lifeline to human civilization at present without which we cannot imagine a day. Do you feel diesel and petrol raging through your veins and roaring noise of engine drives you crazy? Here is a chance to take on the engine for real and show how much you are familiar with it. The event will be conducted in two rounds. Preliminary Round would have written questions based on the practical knowledge about different components of any two or four wheeler. Out of the students who appeared for prelims, 8 teams would be selected for the mains. In the final round, teams would be given practical tasks like assembling piston, crankshaft and head, clutch system, wheel assembly, suspension assembly, etc
Max Participants: 2 per team
